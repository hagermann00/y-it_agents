## Strategic Comparison

The AAA model is evaluated against more stable or higher-probability income streams to provide context for the "Pivot" decision.

| Option | Startup Cost | Failure Rate | Time to Profit |
|--------|--------------|--------------|----------------|
| **AI Agency (AAA)** | $3,000 - $12,000 | 85-90% | 6-18 months |
| **McDonald's / Entry Job** | $0 | 0% | 2 weeks |
| **Index Fund (S&P 500)** | $100+ | ~0% (Long term) | 10-30 years |
| **Freelance Automation** | $0 - $500 | 30-40% | 1-3 months |

## Alternative: Freelance Automation

Instead of building an "Agency" with high overhead (Stack AI, Course fees), a pivot to **Freelance Automation** is recommended for those with 1-2 YES scores on the Gauntlet.

- **What it is:** Selling specific Zapier or Make.com workflows to existing businesses on a per-project basis.
- **Why it works:** No "God Stack" required. Uses tools businesses already pay for.
- **Expectations:** $500 - $2,500 per project.

## Technical Resource: Zapier

The use of Zapier is highlighted as a high-utility resource that allows for lower-overhead automation compared to the full AAA stack. It bypasses the "Hallucination Anxiety" by focusing on deterministic lead flow rather than conversational AI.
