Let’s talk about the entry fee. Before you even talk to a single plumber, you’re already $3,000 in the hole. That’s for the course that told you it was easy. It’s the first "Investment in Your Success," which is guru-speak for "My house payment is now your problem."

But the bleeding doesn't stop there. An agency isn't a business; it's a collection of subscriptions that eat while you sleep.

- **Voiceflow Pro:** $50 a month (just to keep your bots from being homeless).
- **Stack AI:** $150 a month (because the "free" version has the IQ of a toaster).

That’s a $200 monthly burn rate. Before you've made a single dime.

[IMAGE: posibot-ch3-01-right]
*PosiBot holds a sign: "Startup costs are an INVESTMENT in your future! Don't look at the numbers! Look at the PASSION!"*

And then there's the lead gen. You bought $500 worth of leads from Apollo.io because you realized that finding 400 dentists manually is a soul-crushing endeavor.

So, you start sending those DMs we talked about in Chapter 2. The "FOOLPROOF" roadmap.

Here is the data the roadmap forgot to mention: The failure rate for bot-generated DMs is currently tracking at 99.5%. Why? Because business owners now have the same biological reaction to an Instagram DM that a gazelle has to a rustle in the tall grass. They don't want your "Free AI Audit." They want you to leave them alone.

[IMAGE: posibot-ch3-02-left]
*PosiBot holds a sign: "It's a numbers game! If 995 people say no, just find 1,000 more!"*

Chad is doing the math. To just get back to *zero*, he needs to sign a client for $500 a month and keep them for seven months. He also realizes that most plumbers don't stay for seven months if the bot keeps telling customers they're out of business.

The engine is running. But it’s not taking us to the beach. It’s taking us to the basement.
