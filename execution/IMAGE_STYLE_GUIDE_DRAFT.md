## INFOGRAPHIC STYLE

### Design Principles
```
AESTHETIC: Clean, professional, data-driven
VIBE: Business report meets satirical expose
NOT: Clip art, stock photo, cartoon

LAYOUT:
â”œâ”€â”€ White or light gray background
â”œâ”€â”€ Clear visual hierarchy
â”œâ”€â”€ Minimal decoration
â”œâ”€â”€ Data is the star
```

### Typography
```
HEADERS:    Bold sans-serif (Inter, Roboto, or similar)
DATA:       Clear, large numbers
LABELS:     Smaller, readable
EMPHASIS:   Color, not decoration
```
