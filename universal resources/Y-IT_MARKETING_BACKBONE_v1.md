# Y-IT MARKETING BACKBONE v1.0

## The Anti-Guru Promotion Engine

This document defines the templates and frameworks for marketing Y-IT books. It focuses on the "Disruption" of common marketing myths.

---

## 1. THE "STOP" AD FRAMEWORK (Social Media)

**Goal:** Arrest attention by using the data gurus ignore.

### Template 1: The Math Check

- **Headline:** Gurus say $10k/month. The math says -$42/hour.
- **Copy:** We analyzed [Topic] data from 500+ participants. 87% lost money. The median loss was $[Amount]. Want the truth before you burn your savings?
- **CTA:** Read the Body Count. [Link]

### Template 2: The Character Callout

- **Headline:** Are you Tyler or Karen?
- **Copy:** Tyler had $50k and his dad's Shopify contact. Karen had $400 and a dream. Only one of them is on the guru's sales page.
- **CTA:** See the autopsies. [Link]

---

## 2. THE "GUT PUNCH" EMAIL SEQUENCE

**Goal:** Build trust through "Empathetic Brutality."

### Email 1: The First $1,000

- **Hook:** Your first $1,000 in [Topic] is the most expensive money you'll ever make.
- **Body:** Talk about the hidden costs in CH03. Real SaaS fees, ad testing, and the "Expert Tax" (courses).
- **The Data:** Show a comparison of "Projected Costs" vs. "Actual Costs" from the Data Table.

### Email 2: The Survivor Bias

- **Hook:** Why that Lambo isn't yours.
- **Body:** Explain the 2% success rate from CH05. Highlight that the winners usually have "Unfair Advantages."
- **The Data:** List the 4 requirements for success (Capital, Time, Skills, Advantage).
